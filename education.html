    • My Education

    Currently I am studying at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay ,but looking back, I've come a long way.


    I was born and raised in Nagpur, Maharashtra, India; hence, my schooling years were spent in Nagpur. From pre-primary to the final years of my secondary education, I was schooled in SANDIPANI SCHOOL. I had never ever changed schools if not for the branch change from Class 4 to Class 5, where I shifted from SANDIPANI Civil Lines to SANDIPANI HazariPahad.


    After my secondary schooling, I joined the Yashoda College for my junior college studies. As I was preparing for JEE (Main + Advanced), I had to opt for the Science field and I choose my optional BiFocal Subject as Computer Science. I rarely went to college though. During the same time, I had also joined the ICAD institute; it was a coaching class that helped me prepare for my JEE entrance exams.


    After I cleared my entrances, I admitted to the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, where I am still currently studying. I am a B Tech first year student in the Electrical Engineering department as my major.
